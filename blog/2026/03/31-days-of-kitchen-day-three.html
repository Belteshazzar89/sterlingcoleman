    31 Days of the Kitchen: Day Three

    Today was the third day of the kitchen project, but I was in Paris for work all day, and could only work on the project once I got home in the evening. As soon as I came home, I went to the hardware store and picked up some more plastic tubing and plastic anchors to set up electric cables in the basement.

    Armed with those, I got to work drilling holes in the concrete basement ceiling and setting up a few tubes. I fed a couple of cables into the tubes, finishing the path of the two cables I had started yesterday. I started on a new set of cables from the circuit breaker box.

    At that point, I was tired and decided to stop work for the day, rather than risk making a silly mistake. Not much progress today, but that’s all that could be done with limited time and energy.
